Google allows for a limited number of free words every month (counted by characters). This is known as a quota. Once this quota is exceeded, Google starts charging.

The quota counter in this extension is only an approximate count. It's not exact and only gives a rough idea of how many characters have been sent to Google.

Currently, there is no automated way (API) to get the actual current usage from Google.

IMPORTANT Google's quotas and rates are subject to change. The information here may be out of date!
